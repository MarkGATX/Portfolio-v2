About me

I'm a creative full-stack developer and multi-hyphenate who loves learning and creating new things. Conquering good and meaningful challenges is one of my favorite things to do. That and hanging out with my wife and son on our down time.

When it comes to my work, I believe that the experience a person has while using technology should be easy, enjoyable, and memorable. The best measure of success is making people feel a connection. I take that philosophy in all of my work, from development to film.
